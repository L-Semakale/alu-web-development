Frequently Asked Questions

What is gender equality?

Gender equality is the state in which access to rights or opportunities is unaffected by gender.

Why is gender equality important?

Gender equality is essential for the achievement of human rights for all.

How can we promote gender equality?

Promoting gender equality involves addressing and challenging social norms and stereotypes.

What are the benefits of gender equality?

Gender equality leads to significant improvements in various aspects of society, including economic growth and social cohesion.
